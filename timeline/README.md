## 2. Signals to watch

Things that, if they happen, change the decision — not just news.

- **A third delay.** Resets the entire calendar; every date timed off 2026-11-19
  shifts. The single highest-impact signal. Take-Two currently says it will not
  happen — watch each earnings call for confirmation.
- **Metacritic score band at launch.** 90+ sustains a multi-year attention wave;
  75–80 shortens it sharply. Decision-relevant for all of Tier 1.
- **Pre-order numbers / Take-Two guidance.** The first hard demand data — strong
  numbers de-risk Tier 1; weak numbers are a warning.
- **TTWO price action** around earnings and Trailer 3 — relevant if Tier 2 is in
  play; tracked observationally regardless.
- **Crypto macro (BTC).** Tier 2 items 9–11 are highly correlated with the broader
  crypto market; a deep drawdown invalidates that tier independent of GTA 6.
- **Rockstar's stance on RP servers / FiveM for GTA 6.** The Sept 2025 NoPixel
  endorsement is the precedent; whether console RP is supported (≈10× player base)
  materially changes the size of opportunities #6 and #7.
- **Rockstar Terms of Service on crypto / monetization.** Explicit prohibition of
  crypto integration would outright kill opportunity #12. This needs an actual
  reading once GTA 6 ToS is published — currently the blocker on #12.
- **PC modding / SDK signals.** Any official word on modding support timing gates
  opportunity #5.
